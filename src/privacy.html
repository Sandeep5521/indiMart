Privacy Policy

At IndiMart, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users' personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide when using our website and related services. By accessing or using IndiMart, you consent to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1.1 Personal Information: When you create an account or make a purchase on IndiMart, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, shipping address, phone number, and payment details. We will only collect the necessary information required to fulfill your orders and provide you with a personalized shopping experience.

1.2 Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze trends, and gather demographic information. These technologies may collect non-personal information such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited on our website.

  1. Use of Information

2.1 Personalization: We use the information collected to personalize your experience on IndiMart, including displaying relevant products, providing personalized recommendations, and improving our services.

2.2 Order Fulfillment: We use your personal information to process and fulfill your orders, communicate with you about your purchases, and provide customer support.

2.3 Communication: We may send you promotional emails, newsletters, or updates about new products, offers, or events. You have the option to opt-out of receiving such communications by following the instructions provided in the emails or contacting us directly.

2.4 Analytics and Improvements: We may use your information to analyze user behavior, monitor trends, and improve our website, products, and services. This may include conducting surveys or research to enhance user experience and customer satisfaction.

2.5 Legal Compliance: We may use and disclose your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or enforceable governmental requests, as required by law.

3.1 Third-Party Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, processing payments, delivering products, or conducting business activities on our behalf. These service providers are obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information.

3.3 Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or in good faith belief that such disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, comply with a legal obligation, investigate fraud, or ensure the safety of our users.

However, please note that no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
